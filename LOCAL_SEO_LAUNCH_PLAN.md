## 🏢 Week 2–3: Local Business Citations

Citations = mentions of your NAP (Name, Address, Phone) on other websites.
Google uses these to verify you're real.

### Priority 1 (Must Do)

1. **Yelp** [yelp.com/biz/add](https://biz.yelp.com/signup)
   - Business name, address, phone
   - Upload logo
   - Write a 200-word description
   - Add 3–5 photos
   - Fill out all details

2. **Better Business Bureau** [bbb.org](https://www.bbb.org)
   - Create account
   - Verify business
   - Get accredited (if you want)

3. **Apple Maps** [business.apple.com](https://business.apple.com)
   - Submit business info
   - Apple will verify

4. **Angie's List** [angieslist.com](https://www.angieslist.com)
   - Create business profile
   - Submit for verification

### Priority 2 (Should Do)

- HomeAdvisor
- Thumbtack
- Care.com
- Local Chamber of Commerce (Yuma Chamber of Commerce)
- Yuma County Business websites
- Local directories specific to Arizona

### Priority 3 (Nice to Have)

- Facebook Business Page (don't advertise yet; just claim it)
- Instagram Business Account (photos of clean homes)
- Local business aggregator sites

**Critical:** Use EXACT NAP (Name, Address, Phone) everywhere. Inconsistencies confuse Google.

## ⭐ Week 4+: Review Building

Google's ranking algorithm now heavily weights **genuine reviews**. You need real reviews.

### Strategy

1. **In-app request after service**
   - Text or email 24 hours after cleaning: "How did we do? [Link to review]"
   - Make it easy—one click should open Google review page

2. **Thank you cards**
   - Handwritten note in client invoices: "We'd love your feedback on Google!"
   - Include QR code linking to Google review

3. **Follow-up email**
   - 48 hours after service
   - "Love working with you? [Review us here]"
   - Make the link obvious

4. **Never pay for reviews**
   - Google penalizes fake/paid reviews
   - Genuine 3-star reviews are better than fake 5-stars

### Goal

- **Month 1:** 5–10 real reviews
- **Month 2:** 15–20 reviews
- **Month 3:** 30+ reviews, 4.5+ average rating

Each review signals to Google: "This company is real and people like them."

## 🔗 Month 3+: Backlink Building

Backlinks = other websites linking to you. Google treats these as "votes."

### Strategy

1. **Local directories & sponsorships**
   - Sponsor local youth sports team (website mentions your business)
   - Join Chamber of Commerce (get listed on website)
   - Contribute to local nonprofit (they link to supporters)

2. **Local press mentions**
   - Email local news: "Local business owner shares haboob cleanup tips"
   - Offer to be quoted in "How Yuma Residents Deal With Dust" articles
   - Reach out to Yuma Sun, Yuma county blogs

3. **Guest posts**
   - Write a guest post for local business blogs
   - Include a link back to your site

4. **Listing sites**
   - GetYellowPages, MerchantCircle, etc. (they provide backlinks)

5. **Local partnerships**
   - Partner with property managers
   - Partner with real estate agents
   - Partner with moving companies
   - (These often link to referral partners)

## 🚫 Things NOT to Do

1. **Don't buy fake reviews** — Google will penalize you
2. **Don't overuse keywords** — "cleaning Yuma cleaning cleaning Yuma" looks spammy
3. **Don't skip Google Business Profile** — 60% of local searches end in Google Maps
4. **Don't hide citations** — Use exact NAP everywhere
5. **Don't neglect local content** — Generic cleaning guides don't rank as well
6. **Don't pay for backlinks** — Google penalizes paid link schemes
