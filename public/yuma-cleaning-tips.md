# Yuma Cleaning Tips Card

## Hard Water & Desert Dust in Yuma

**Hard Water Spotting**
- Our Colorado River water is mineral-heavy
- Hard-water deposits build up on fixtures, glass, shower doors
- Quarterly deep cleans help prevent permanent staining
- Vinegar & microfiber cloths are your friends between cleanings

**Desert Dust Management**
- Fine Yuma dust settles 1.5x faster than other Arizona cities
- Bi-weekly cleaning beats monthly in our climate
- During monsoon (June–Sept), consider weekly service
- Regular cleaning prevents dust from getting ground in

**Seasonal Adjustments**
- **May–June:** Book a pre-season deep clean before haboob season
- **June–September:** Step up to bi-weekly or weekly during monsoon
- **October–April:** Monthly maintenance usually sufficient

**Between Cleanings**
- Dust high-traffic areas weekly (entryways, sills)
- Wipe down fixtures weekly to prevent hard-water spotting
- Vacuum baseboards where Yuma dust collects fastest
- Check AC vents and return-air grilles monthly

**Deep Clean Focus**
- Grout & tile lines (mineral deposits set in deep)
- Shower glass & fixtures (hard-water spotting)
- Window tracks & sills (dust accumulation)
- Baseboards & corners (where fine dust settles)
